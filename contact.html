GST registration is generally mandatory if your aggregate annual turnover exceeds ₹40 Lakhs (for goods) or ₹20 Lakhs (for services) in normal states. Some special category states have lower thresholds. However, certain inter-state businesses and e-commerce sellers require mandatory registration regardless of turnover.
